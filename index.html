About Me

How it began


I'm a designer and developer living in Ontario, Canada. I love to solve problems, build and rearrange things. Coming from DR Congo, creativity was abundant which steered me towards the path of creating and editing videos for several purposes. Having sharpened my skills over the years, I later decided to do work for others instead, in order to learn about how to make everyone else's experience as good as mine or even better

In addition to my design oriented background, I've also developped coding skills and gained further knowledge on software such as Cinema 4D, Photoshop, Premiere Pro and various other Creative Cloud software The best part about this industry is that you're never really done learning. There's always room for growth and opportunities

My Hobbies


When I'm not coding or designing anything I'm thinking of ways to upgrade my most prized project, my arcade machine! Building it was the fun part because now that it's finished I never have time to play. I'm very passionate about retro games and all things arcade, and I'm also a HUGE Mortal Kombat and South Park fan!
